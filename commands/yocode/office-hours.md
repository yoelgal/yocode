# /yocode:office-hours

Product thinking mode — two flavors.

## Mode Selection

Ask: "Is this for a startup/business or a personal/side project?"

---

## Startup Mode: 6 Forcing Questions

For products trying to find market fit.

### Q1: Demand Reality
"Who is desperate for this? Not 'would use it' — desperate.
Name a specific person or company. What are they doing today instead?"

### Q2: Status Quo
"What's the current workaround? How painful is it?
If the pain isn't bad enough to Google for solutions, it's not bad enough to build for."

### Q3: Desperate Specificity
"Describe your ideal first user. Not a persona — a real person.
What's their job title? What did they Google this morning? What Slack channels are they in?"

### Q4: Narrowest Wedge
"What is the absolute smallest version of this that would make someone switch from their current solution?
Not the vision. The wedge. The thing so good at one specific job that they can't say no."

### Q5: Observation & Surprise
"What have you observed about this space that surprised you?
Not what you assumed — what you SAW that contradicted your assumptions."

### Q6: Future-Fit
"In 3 years, what's changed about the world that makes this 10x more valuable?
What tailwind are you riding?"

After all 6: Synthesize into a product brief with honest assessment.

---

## Builder Mode: Design Thinking

For side projects, hackathons, and creative exploration.

### Phase 1: Empathize
- Who is this for?
- What's their day look like?
- What frustrates them?

### Phase 2: Define
- What's the core problem?
- What's the insight that makes this different?
- One sentence: "This helps [who] do [what] by [how]."

### Phase 3: Ideate
- Generate 5 wildly different approaches
- For each: what's the best case? What's the risk?
- Rank by: excitement × feasibility

### Phase 4: Prototype Plan
- What's the smallest thing you can build to test the idea?
- What would convince you it's working?
- What's the timeline? (Be honest.)

### Phase 5: Decision
- Build it? (Yes / Not yet / No)
- If yes: hand off to `/yocode:plan`
- If not yet: create a seed for when conditions change
- If no: document why (your future self will thank you)
